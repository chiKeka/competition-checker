### 1. IP & Ownership — 7?/10
Worldwide royalty-free promotional license covering code, concepts, workflows, and founder likenesses; no restriction on sponsor building competing products

- > "does not restrict Perplexity, Perplexity Fund, or their affiliates from independently developing, creating, investing in, or pursuing products, services, companies, or ideas similar to or competitive with any submission."
  `[T&C, IP section, as reported by BusinessToday]` — no restriction on sponsor developing competing products or services

- > "a worldwide, royalty-free promotional licence to use their company name, product, code, concepts, workflows, founder likenesses and demo footage for marketing, investor relations, and other commercial purposes without further consent or compensation."
  `[T&C, promotional license clause, as reported by BusinessToday]` — worldwide, royalty-free, covers code and concepts, no consent required

### 2. Equity & Financial — 7/10
Investment framed as prize but is not a prize; requires Delaware C-Corp incorporation; acceptance of equity terms is a condition of receiving funds

- > "Perplexity Fund is under no obligation to invest in any participant. Investment amounts, if any, are determined at Perplexity Fund's sole discretion."
  `[T&C, investment section]` — no obligation to invest

- > "Perplexity Fund can invest in 'zero, one, two, three, or more companies — or no companies at all — at its sole discretion.'"
  `[T&C, §13 as reported by BusinessToday]` — number of winners at sole discretion

- Investment is contingent on: due diligence, incorporating as a US Delaware C-Corp, a mutually agreed investment agreement, and compliance with full T&Cs.
  `[T&C, investment conditions section]` — mandatory Delaware C-Corp incorporation

### 3. Exclusivity & Restraint — 4?/10
Mandatory use of Perplexity Computer platform during competition; no explicit non-compete or fundraising freeze identified

- Participants must hold an active Perplexity Max or Pro subscription and use Perplexity Computer to build their product during the 8-week competition period.
  `[T&C, eligibility section]` — mandatory platform usage

- No explicit non-compete, fundraising freeze, or prohibition on entering other competitions was identified in the available T&C text. Coverage was partial.
  `[Not found in available sources]`

### 4. Publicity & Data — 8/10
Perpetual royalty-free license over founder likenesses, demos, and company name for marketing; full access to all participant queries and workflow data during competition

- > "a worldwide, royalty-free promotional licence to use their company name, product, code, concepts, workflows, founder likenesses and demo footage for marketing, investor relations, and other commercial purposes without further consent or compensation."
  `[T&C, promotional license clause, as reported by BusinessToday]` — perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free, covers likenesses, no opt-out specified

- > "retains the right to access and review all Perplexity Computer queries, prompts, task outputs, workflow data and related account activity of registered participants during the competition period."
  `[T&C, data access clause, as reported by BusinessToday]` — full access to all participant platform activity, described as a condition of participation

  `[T&C, timeline section]` — 8-week build period with traction deliverables
